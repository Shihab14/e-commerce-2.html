About Us

Calzone is a global e-commerce company that sells a wide variety of products and services. We were founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos and our mission is to be Earth's most customer-centric company.

We offer a wide variety of products and services, including books, music, movies, electronics, clothing, and more. We also offer a variety of shipping options, including free two-day shipping for Prime members.

We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible shopping experience. That's why we offer a wide variety of products, low prices, and excellent customer service.

We are proud to be a part of the global community and we are committed to making a positive impact on the world. We are always looking for ways to improve our products and services and to make our company more sustainable.

We are grateful for the opportunity to serve our customers and we look forward to continuing to grow and innovate in the years to come.
